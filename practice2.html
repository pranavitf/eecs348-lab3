Tennis is a globally celebrated racket sport known for its elegance and intensity. Played between two opponents or two teams of two, the objective is to score points by hitting a ball over a net into the opponent's court. The game demands a remarkable combination of agility, power, and strategic thinking from its players. Major tournaments, known as Grand Slams—Wimbledon, the US Open, the French Open, and the Australian Open—are the pinnacles of the sport. Legendary rivalries and iconic athletes have shaped its rich history, captivating millions of fans worldwide. It remains a popular recreational activity and a thrilling professional spectacle.
